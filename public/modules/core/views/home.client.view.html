What is Fitty?

Welcome to Fitty, the personal trainer you carry in your pocket! Fitty helps you schedule when to workout, what to workout, and how to workout. With notifications reminding you when your workouts are, a plan for each workout, and instructions on how to perform each routine safely, you’re out of excuses! Let’s get Fit, with Fitty!
